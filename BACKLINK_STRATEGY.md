## üéØ Phase 1: Search Engine Submission (Week 1)

### 1. Bing Webmaster Tools
**Priority:** HIGH | **Time:** 15 minutes | **Impact:** High visibility in Bing search

**Steps:**
1. Visit: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
2. Sign in with Microsoft account (create if needed)
3. Click "Add a site"
4. Enter: `https://magicpencil.fun`
5. Choose verification method: **XML file verification** (easiest)
   - Download `BingSiteAuth.xml` file
   - Upload to root directory of website
   - Click "Verify"
6. Submit sitemap: `https://magicpencil.fun/sitemap.xml`
7. Set site preferences:
   - Target country: United States (or your target)
   - Target audience: General
8. Enable crawl rate: Normal

**Expected Result:** Bing will index your site within 1-2 weeks

---

### 2. Yandex Webmaster
**Priority:** MEDIUM | **Time:** 15 minutes | **Impact:** International visibility

**Steps:**
1. Visit: https://webmaster.yandex.com/
2. Click "Add site"
3. Enter: `https://magicpencil.fun`
4. Verify ownership:
   - Choose "Meta tag" method
   - Add meta tag to `<head>` of index.html:
     ```html
     <meta name="yandex-verification" content="YOUR_CODE_HERE" />
     ```
5. Submit sitemap: `https://magicpencil.fun/sitemap.xml`

**Why Yandex?**
- Good for international audience (Russia, Eastern Europe)
- Helps with overall domain credibility
- Fast indexing

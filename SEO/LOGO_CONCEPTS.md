## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Favicon (16x16px, 32x32px)
**Use:** `magicpencil-logo-minimal.svg` (Letter M version)
**Reason:** Simple shapes work best at tiny sizes

### Header Logo (Desktop)
**Use:** `magicpencil-logo-primary.svg` + "MagicPencil" text
**Size:** ~40-60px height

### Header Logo (Mobile)
**Use:** `magicpencil-logo-minimal.svg` only (no text)
**Size:** ~32-40px height

### Social Media (Facebook, Twitter)
**Use:** `magicpencil-logo-primary.svg` (square crop)
**Size:** 400x400px minimum

### Open Graph Image
**Use:** `magicpencil-logo-primary.svg` + tagline on colored background
**Size:** 1200x630px
